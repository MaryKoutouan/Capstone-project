About Us

This website is about showing a list of organizations to other women where they can have a coding BootCamp is the primary purpose of this project. In this app, they can have a piece of overall information on each Bootcamp by selecting on the name or image of this one.

They will be able to interact by adding the name of a Bootcamp that doesn't yet exist in the list, able to click on the direct link of each organization if they want more information, able to give some reviews and rating the Bootcamp.
